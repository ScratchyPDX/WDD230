Target Audience

The target audience for this website are growers of fresh fruits and vegetables, individuals who are looking for same.

Persona #1

"Mac" McKight owns and operates a berry growing and processing business. While he is a successful grower, he is looking partner to help get his produce to local and national markets.

Persona #2

Julia is very health conscious and her diet includes a lot of fruits and vegetables. The fresher the produce, the greater the health benefit. Julia is looking for a vendor that is close to the grower, insuring the things she buys are a fresh as possible.

Persona #3

Michael like fruit drinks. Sure they are healthy, but he really likes the taste. Michael leads a busy lifestyle, so online ordering is convenient and quick. He also want to ability to change up the fruity mix from time to time so a variety of choices is a must.
