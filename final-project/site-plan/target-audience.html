Target Audience

The target audience for this website are all the businesses in Troutdale and the surrounding communities.

Persona #1

Michael runs a construction and subcontracting company. He want to use his association with the Chamber of Commerce to extend his business contact, hoping that this will attract new business opportunities and build his business.

Persona #2

Julia is a small business owner on Main Street. Her shop does most of it business during the summer months when tourism is high. She is interested in what civic events the Chamber of Commerce is planning that will draw more tourists to town.

Scenarios

  • How will the Chamber support better business relations with government?
  • How will promoted activities tht lead to economic development?
  • What can the Chamber do to promote tourism?
