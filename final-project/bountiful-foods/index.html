Bountiful Foods

“Volume without profit is like eating soup with a fork: you don't get very full.” Bountiful Food will help you find that balance. When you are healthy, we are healthy.

Our goal has always been to provide value-added products to local independent markets. We deal directly with growers to assist them in marketing their fresh apples, cherries and soft fruit, as well as vegetables, directly to their customers. The fewer steps between the farmer and the end-user, the more profit in your pocket.
