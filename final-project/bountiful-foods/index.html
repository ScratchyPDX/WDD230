Bountiful Foods Co.

It all started back in 1911 when Agostino John Papadopoulos immigrated to Carlsbad from Greece and started delivering produce house to house. His business was passed onto his son Agostino Papadopoulos (better known as "Poppy"), thus produce has been a part of Poppy's life since birth. This tradition is one that we are proud of at Bountiful Food Co. remains a family business.

Through the years Poppy (and family) has purchased the following companies: Baccelieri Produce, Danna Produce, Rainbow Produce, Wilson Produce, Seitz Produce, and SoCal Meats.
