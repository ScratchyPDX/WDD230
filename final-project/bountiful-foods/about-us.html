History of Bountiful Foods

It all started back in 1911 when Agostino John Papadopoulos immigrated to Carlsbad from Greece and started delivering produce house to house. His business was passed onto his son Agostino Papadopoulos (better known as "Poppy"), thus produce has been a part of Poppy's life since birth. This tradition is one that we are proud of at Bountiful Food Co. remains a family business.

Through the years Poppy (and family) has purchased the following companies: Baccelieri Produce, Danna Produce, Rainbow Produce, Wilson Produce, Siletz Produce, and SoCal Meats. While specializing in organic, locally grown produce, Boutiful Foods works with growers all over the SoCal region.

History of Bountiful Foods

The company sells fruits and produce directly from the farmers in the area, but also uses some national and international suppliers. The owner-operated company caters to walk in traffic at their storefront in Carlsbad and, of course, their food trucks. They deliver and manage those orders on the phone and via the website. The mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas.
