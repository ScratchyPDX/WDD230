History of Bountiful Foods

It all started back in 1911 when Agostino John Papadopoulos immigrated to Carlsbad from Greece and started delivering produce house to house. His business was passed onto his son Agostino Papadopoulos (better known as "Poppy"), thus produce has been a part of Poppy's life since birth. This tradition is one that we are proud of at Bountiful Food Co. remains a family business.

Through the years Poppy (and family) has purchased the following companies: Baccelieri Produce, Danna Produce, Rainbow Produce, Wilson Produce, Siletz Produce, and SoCal Meats.

Grow Local, Sell Local

While specializing in organic, locally grown produce, Bountiful Foods works with growers all over the SoCal region. Bountiful Foods sells fruits and produce directly from the farmers in the area, but also uses some national and international suppliers.

The owner-operated company caters to walk in traffic at our storefront in Carlsbad and, of course, our food trucks. They deliver and manage those orders on the phone and via the website. Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the SocCal coastal areas.

Farm Produce On-The-Go

Bountiful Foods is physically located in Carlsbad, California. However, we have many food trucks that cover the coastal areas from Los Angeles to San Diego.

The idea of farm to food truck isn't a new concept to the mobile food industry, but at the same time, it is a term that some food truck owners aren't aware of. This is a concept of purchasing locally grown food directly from the source.
